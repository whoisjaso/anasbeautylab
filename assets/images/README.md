# Image Assets Required

## Hero Section
- `hero-poster.jpg` - Video poster image (1920x1080px)
  - Show macro shot of glowing skin or serum
  - High quality, luxury feel

## About Section
- `ana-portrait.jpg` - Portrait of Ana (800x1000px, portrait orientation)
  - Professional headshot or working shot
  - Warm, inviting expression

## Services (600x400px each)
- `facial-service.jpg` - Signature facial treatment
- `lash-service.jpg` - Lash lift/tint service
- `peel-service.jpg` - Chemical peel treatment
- `micro-service.jpg` - Microdermabrasion treatment

## Gallery
- `gallery-1.jpg` - Studio interior/sanctuary shot
- `gallery-2.jpg` - Product collection/lifestyle
- `gallery-3.jpg` - Client transformation/treatment
- `og-image.jpg` - Social share image (1200x630px)

## Cinemagraphs (Video)
- `serum-cinemagraph.mp4` - Slow-motion serum drops or texture
- `steam-cinemagraph.mp4` - Rising steam from treatment

## Naming Convention for SEO
All images should have descriptive filenames including location:
- Good: `facial-treatment-houston-tx.jpg`
- Good: `lash-lift-houston-anas-beauty-lab.jpg`
- Bad: `IMG_1234.jpg`

## Image Optimization
- Format: JPG for photos, WebP if supported
- Quality: 80-85% for web
- Max width: 1920px for hero, 800px for others
- Use TinyPNG or Squoosh for compression

## Alt Text Examples
```html
<img src="facial-service.jpg" alt="Signature facial treatment at Ana's Beauty Lab Houston TX - luxury skincare studio">
<img src="lash-service.jpg" alt="Lash lift and tint service Houston Texas - Ana's Beauty Lab professional esthetician">
```
