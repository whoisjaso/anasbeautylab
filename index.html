Beauty Intelligence

Expert answers to your Houston skincare questions

How to protect skin from Houston humidity?

In Houston's humid climate, protecting your skin requires a strategic approach. The combination of heat and moisture can lead to clogged pores and increased oil production. We recommend lightweight, gel-based moisturizers that hydrate without heaviness. Our Houston-specific treatments incorporate oil-controlling ingredients while maintaining essential moisture balance.

What is the best facial for Houston weather?

Houston's subtropical climate demands facials that address both hydration and oil control. Our Signature Glow Facial is specifically designed for this environment, featuring enzymes that gently exfoliate without stripping, combined with hyaluronic acid for deep hydration. Regular monthly treatments help maintain optimal skin health year-round.

How often should you get a facial in Houston?

In Houston's climate, we recommend monthly professional facials to counteract the effects of heat, humidity, and urban pollution. This frequency allows for proper skin cell turnover while addressing seasonal changes. During summer months, more frequent exfoliation treatments may be beneficial for controlling excess oil and preventing breakouts.

Best skincare routine for Texas heat?

A Texas heat skincare routine starts with a gentle, non-stripping cleanser, followed by antioxidant serum (vitamin C is ideal), lightweight moisturizer, and SPF 50+ sunscreen. In the evening, incorporate retinol or peptides for repair. Our estheticians customize product recommendations based on your specific skin type and Houston's seasonal variations.
